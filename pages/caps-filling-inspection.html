Caps Filling Inspection

Key Features:

Counting different Caps moving on a rotating machine.

Identifying Color of Caps (Red, White, Blue, Black & Gray).

Checking whether the Caps have properly placed Seal or not.

Checking whether the ring over the cap is deformed or not.

Identifying defected Caps and stopping the machine once defected cap is detected

Using multiple machine and deep learning models to improve accuracy.

Calculating Diameter of each Cap.
